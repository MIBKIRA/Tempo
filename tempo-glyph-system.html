Milestone reuses the same green as the Evening Review success state (#34D399) — it already means "completed" everywhere else, so it means the same thing here instead of introducing a new "celebration colour."

Settings tab list needs no new icon at all. Every entry in sidebarNavItems already carries a real lucide icon alongside a redundant emoji field, and two section headers (Profile, Notifications) print the emoji directly in the heading text. That's cleanup, not design — remove the emoji field and the inline emoji in those two headings, keep the lucide icon that's already doing the job.

19 glyphs total now — the 12 from before, plus Rain/Ocean/Forest/Café and Insight/Milestone. That's every repeated emoji found across the app. One-off decorative uses (if any turn up during the sweep) should follow this same grammar rather than get invented fresh.

Every chip has a copy svg button in the top-right corner (hover to see it) — copies that icon's raw markup for whoever's wiring this in.
