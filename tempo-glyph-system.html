What this covers, what it doesn't yet. These 12 glyphs handle the highest-traffic emoji — the ones repeated across TodayView, WeekView, CalendarView, EnergyPlannerView, and EveningReview. Left untouched for now: the ambient-sound icons in FocusMode (🌧 🌊 🌲 ☕), the redundant emoji on the Settings tab list (it already has real lucide icons sitting right next to them — those emoji look like dead leftovers, worth confirming before designing anything new for them), and a handful of one-off alert emoji (🚀 🙌 💡). Same grammar, same corner-chip housing — just not drawn yet, pending your go-ahead on this direction first.

Every chip has a copy svg button in the top-right corner (hover to see it) — copies that icon's raw markup for whoever's wiring this in.
